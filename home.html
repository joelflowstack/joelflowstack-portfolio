What this studio does

Four disciplines, one build process.

Most agencies split "the visual thing" from "the technical thing." This one doesn't — because the most interesting projects need an interface that feels alive, backed by systems that actually work.

01

AI agents

Chatbots and assistants wired to production LLM infrastructure with actual context, memory, and automatic reliability failover — not a scripted FAQ bot pretending to be smart. Flow V3, running in the corner of this page, is a live example.

03

Discord, Telegram, and WhatsApp bots for communities and support desks — moderation, onboarding, ticket routing, and always-on responses that don't feel like a script.

How a project runs

Four stages, no committee.

1. Scope

A short conversation to pin down what the site or bot actually needs to do — not a 40-slide discovery deck. Usually resolved in a single call or thread.

2. Build in the open

Work happens directly on GitHub, deployed to Vercel on every commit. You can watch progress happen in real time instead of waiting for a big reveal.

3. Review & correct

Feedback gets folded in fast — usually same day. No layers of account management between a note you leave and the fix landing.

4. Ship & support

Launch isn't the finish line. Bots keep learning, sites keep getting new sections, and something is usually one Slack message away from being fixed.
