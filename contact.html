Frequently asked

Or skip reading and just ask Flow directly — the chat button in the corner is the same AI behind the Flow V3 project on the Work page.

How fast do you turn projects around?

A first working preview usually ships within days, not weeks — everything deploys live via GitHub + Vercel as it's built, so you're looking at the real thing early, not a mockup.

What does a project cost?

Depends entirely on scope — a single bot integration and a full 3D site with automation behind it are very different builds. Tell me what you need in the form above and I'll come back with a real number, not a range.

Do you work outside web development?

Yes — bot automation (Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp) and n8n workflow builds are just as central as the web work. Most projects end up touching at least two of the three.

Can you take over an existing site or bot?

Usually, yes. Send the repo or a link to what exists now and I'll tell you honestly whether it's worth extending or rebuilding.

Is Flow (the chat widget) actually AI, or scripted replies?

It's the real thing — the same multi-provider AI chain (Cerebras/Groq/OpenRouter) behind the Flow V3 project, answering live. Not a canned FAQ bot.

Do you sign NDAs / handle confidential projects?

Yes, no issue there — mention it when you reach out and I'll send one over before we get into specifics.
