What should I include in my first message?

Roughly what you're trying to build, any deadline you're working against, and whether you already have a GitHub/Vercel setup or need one from scratch. Everything else can be figured out in the reply.

Do you take on small, single-feature jobs?

Yes — plenty of work is a single bot, a single automation, or one new section of an existing site. It doesn't need to be a full rebuild to be worth reaching out about.
